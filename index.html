Opened - around March 1, 2022. Located at Hill Station, Old school junction, Freetown. Several visits under my belt during various parts of the day. Hagar, the manager, is a fantastic you lady, and her crew just as fantastic! Truthfully, we've never had a bad experience here. Ahhh, the name "Hagar's kitchen"....

Enjoyed up thoughts of Brooklyn style pizza, Shawarma, and lots African dishes. Order what you want in red, pink or alfredo sauce. Hagar will ensure your food is made to your liking! If not, she has no problem re-making your food. Hot foods are served hot and cold foods cold; there is no in-between. Fave dishes are the lasagna, baked ziti, manicotti and cheese ravioli. There are daily lunch specials on the sauce - pasta, salad and a drink.

  • We prepare all favourite meals
  • You can have a taste of all yummys
  • We also prepare African dishes like; Cassava leaf, Lafidi, Cassave & beans(mama put am fine)

There are also specials listed on the side of the Coca-Cola machine - 2 slices of pizza and a drink; 1 slice of pizza, salad and a drink; and one with pizza rolls (try the spinach!). There are pizza carry-out specials, though we dine-in the majority of the time. The restaurant and restrooms are clean and up to VIP standards. There are tables for two and four people; however, Hagar will gladly push tables together for larger number of diners. There are also a couple tables outside to dine. You do not bus your table; the staff will do it after you leave. Drinks are self-serve. They also offer two-litre bottles. They do not serve alcohol and I've yet to see anyone. There are also two large screen TVs mounted on the wall; sometimes they show the same channel and some times not. The shows have always been family-friendly. Hagar's Kitchen is like any other restaurant - it has peak lunch and dinner business. With that, keep in mind they are making your food as you requested it. If you don't feel like waiting 10-15 minutes then I strongly suggest to save the diners from your impatience and call in a pick-up/to-go order. Like-wise, if you have a large party, Hagar and crew strive to serve your entire party at the same time. The final decision (like it was questionable?) - Hagar's Kitchen's Pizza and Pasta is definitely HOT!
